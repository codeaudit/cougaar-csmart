A Kinetic impact effects all agents with a radius of the center of the impact. The size of the radius cooresponds to the intensity if the impact.

Latitude

 Latitude of the center of the impact

Longitude

 Longitude of the center of the impact

Time

 Time is the time, in milliseconds, when the impact starts.

Type

 Type is the type of impact. Currently there are 3 defined kinetic impacts: "H2O Flood", "Bomb blast", "Earthquake"

Intensity

 Intensity defines the intensity of the impact, the larger the number, the more severe the impact. Be carefule when assigning an intensity, what may seem like a low intensity may actually be a much larger event. See the CSMART Users Guide for detailed information.

Duration

 Duration is the length of the impact.
